LIFTING CLUB

The Lifting Club is our in person, face to face group coaching programme which is held at the University Of South Wales at their elite training facility. This is an amazing opportunity for anyone who wants to experience what it’s like training as part of a professional sports team.

The Lifting Club runs as a full 8 week training block and is for anyone who wants to improve physically. We focus on getting people stronger, faster and more powerful than ever before. You don’t have to play rugby to get involved, we have had regular gym goers, rugby players and football players attend, both female and male. Both younger and older athletes are more than welcome and we can tailor your programmes accordingly

Outside of rugby or contact sports in general, we also see this as an opportunity for those who want to become part of an amazing community and learn what it’s like to be part of a hard working, goal driven, professional training environment.

The LC is run by Chris Tombs, the head coach at SW7 Academy. Chris has 20+ years coaching elite sports working with Cardiff Rugby, Leicester Tigers, Seattle Seawolves MLR, USA Women's 7’s and USA Womens 15’s consultant. Chris oversaw the development and programming of the likes of British & Irish Lions Sam Warburton, Leigh Halfpenny and Jamie Roberts from the academy and into their professional careers also. Alongside Chris will be some additional coaches that he has hand picked to work with him. All members are provided with a full training programme to follow not only in the sessions but to do in their own time. They also get access to the SW7 Academy App so they can get all their nutrition in order as well.

We have full confidence that our programmes will help any and everyone who follows them to perform better on the pitch! All programs are hosted on our app where players can view and track their workouts.

We have gone all out to provide an elite level service here and as a part of that each member gets a recovery protein shake per session supplied by Pro Athlete Supplements (PAS) and a Canterbury Lifting Club T-shirt.

We have exclusive team memberships available and discounts vary depending on the number of teams or players that a team has. There is a minimum amount of players needed to start achieving the discounted pricing which is 15 players. They would have full access to the academy, the app and all the support available.
